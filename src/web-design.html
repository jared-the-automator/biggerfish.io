What is no-code web design?

No-code web design lets us build sleek, high-performing websites faster and more cost-effectively. At Bigger Fish, we handle the design and development, delivering a site tailored to your business. Once it’s live, you’ll have the power to make updates easily—no coding or waiting on developers. This means you stay in control without sacrificing quality or speed.

Some platforms we know:

  • Webflow is ideal for creating custom, visually stunning websites with advanced design flexibility. It combines creative freedom with clean, production-ready code.
  • Softr builds internal tools, client portals, or lightweight web apps using Airtable or Google Sheets. It’s a user-friendly platform for turning data into functional apps.
  • Squarespace is great for creating beautiful, content-driven websites for creatives and small businesses. It’s an all-in-one platform with built-in hosting and professional templates.
  • Wix is a simple solution for building websites for small businesses or personal projects. Its drag-and-drop interface and wide range of templates make it easy to use.
  • Shopify is designed for e-commerce stores selling physical or digital products. It provides robust tools for managing inventory, payments, and shipping.
  • Carrd is perfect for creating one-page websites like portfolios, landing pages, or personal projects. It’s affordable, quick to set up, and easy to use.
  • WordPress is a scalable platform for building websites with extensive customization and plugin options. Its open-source nature supports a massive ecosystem of themes and tools.
  • GoHighLevel is an all-in-one marketing and CRM platform for agencies and service businesses. It combines funnels, email marketing, and client management in one tool.
  • Mastermind is designed for creating and managing online courses or membership sites. It offers streamlined tools for building and monetizing educational content.
  • Figma is a collaborative design tool for creating user interfaces, prototypes, and design systems. It’s cloud-based, enabling real-time collaboration and seamless sharing.

These platforms also integrate with your existing tools, like CRMs or payment systems, ensuring your website works seamlessly with your operations. It’s a smarter, more flexible way to manage your online presence. Ready for a website that grows with your business? Let’s cook one up together.
