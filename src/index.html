CONTACT // WHY // BRG 090°

Running dark.

Most operators navigate without instruments. No single view of the business, tools that refuse to talk to each other, and processes shaped by what the software allows instead of what the work requires. The friction concentrates in three places.

01 // Dead Reckoning

You navigate by last known position.

Your CRM doesn't talk to your invoicing app. Your invoicing app doesn't talk to your calendar. You estimate where you are from where you were, and the gap grows every week.

02 // Triple Log

Same entry, three different charts.

The lead goes into the CRM, then the schedule, then the invoice. Manual every time. The logs drift apart, and a clear picture of your own business takes assembling by hand.

03 // The Blind Spot

Once the deal is signed, where does it go?

The workarounds pile up until they become the process. You would change how you handle onboarding, but the form has no field for it. The software runs you.
