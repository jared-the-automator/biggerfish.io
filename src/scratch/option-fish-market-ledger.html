The systems most operators inherit were not built for them. They were built for a hundred-thousand-seat customer the operator will never resemble. The workarounds you have invented to make those systems fit your work are a kind of authorship. You are already a designer. We just bring better tools.

What follows is the shape of the work, in two ways. One is bounded, drawn, delivered. The other is ongoing, responsive, retained. The studio runs both at once for different clients, and either is a good answer depending on where you are in the year.

Two ways in.

Most studios make you flatten your business into their schema. We start with how you actually run yours, draw it on a single sheet, and decide together what gets built. The drawing is yours either way.
