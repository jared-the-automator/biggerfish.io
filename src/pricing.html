How much does it cost?

At Bigger Fish, our pricing reflects the complexity of your solution and the value we deliver. We aim for a minimum of 3x ROI within one year, ensuring your investment drives measurable results.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from small operations to large enterprises. As a starting point, single-page websites begin at $599, and RAG Chatbots start at $1199.

To get started on your project, we require 50% of the buildout cost upfront. The remaining 50% will be due upon completion, ensuring everything meets your expectations.

For solutions requiring ongoing service, a monthly fee equal to 10% of the buildout cost covers automation upkeep, updates, and operations.
