Tools of the trade

This page elaborates on some of the no-code development platforms we use to build your bespoke automations for those who are curious. Why would we reveal the inner workings of our business, when people could learn to build the automations themselves?

The answer is explained with a simple analogy. You can surely change the oil in your own car, but do you? Learning any one of these platforms in isolation is like changing your own oil. Hiring Bigger Fish is like getting an oil change plus your tires rotated, fluids topped off, filters replaced, and a multi-point inspection. Learning to do all this is a full-time job. Leave it to us, you've got bigger fish to fry.

A few of our favorite things

  • Airtable is a flexible database platform that combines the simplicity of spreadsheets with the power of relational databases. It’s highly customizable, allowing users to organize and manage data for projects, workflows, or operations. Benefits include its user-friendly interface, extensive templates, and integrations with other tools. Airtable is ideal for teams that need a centralized, collaborative space to manage data and processes.
  • Make.com (formerly Integromat) is a powerful automation platform that connects apps and services to streamline workflows. It allows users to create custom integrations and automate repetitive tasks without coding. With its visual interface, you can map out workflows step-by-step, connecting tools like CRMs, email platforms, and databases. Benefits include saving time on manual processes, reducing errors, and improving operational efficiency. It’s ideal for businesses looking to automate complex workflows across multiple tools.
  • n8n is an open-source workflow automation tool that allows users to connect apps, services, and APIs to automate tasks and processes. It offers a highly customizable platform with a visual workflow editor, making it easy to create complex automations tailored to specific business needs. Benefits include flexibility, self-hosting options for full data control, and the ability to integrate with hundreds of tools and APIs. n8n.io is ideal for businesses seeking a cost-effective, scalable solution for automating workflows without vendor lock-in.
  • Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search and answer engine designed to provide quick, accurate responses to complex queries. It combines natural language processing with real-time data retrieval to deliver concise, reliable answers, often with cited sources for transparency. Benefits include saving time on research, improving decision-making with accurate information, and simplifying access to knowledge. Perplexity AI is ideal for businesses and individuals looking to streamline research and get actionable insights quickly.
  • Softr is a no-code platform for building progressive web apps, internal tools, and client portals using Airtable or Google Sheets as a backend. It’s designed for non-technical users, offering pre-built templates and drag-and-drop functionality to create apps quickly. Benefits include ease of use, fast deployment, and seamless integration with existing data sources. Softr is perfect for businesses that need lightweight, functional apps without the cost or complexity of traditional development.
  • Typeform is an online form builder that creates interactive, user-friendly forms, surveys, and quizzes. Its conversational design keeps users engaged, leading to higher response rates compared to traditional forms. Benefits include customizable templates, seamless integrations with tools like CRMs and email platforms, and detailed analytics to track responses. Typeform is perfect for businesses looking to collect data, feedback, or leads in a way that feels personal and engaging.
